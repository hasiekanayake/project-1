OUR HISTORY



Established in 2005, Ekanayake Tyre Mart has proudly served our local community for 18 years. Throughout our journey, we're evolved to meet ever-changing need of our customers. We began with a passion for providing top-quality tyres and have since expanded our services to include batteries and oil changes Our history is a testament to our commitment to excellence and a reflection of the trust our customers have placed in us.
